EMI'S SERENITY HAVEN


Vivid Reverie     





Walk Away

Leave if your presence is not appreciated

Believe it or not

It is one of the God's plan

To keep you safe

Veiled Lies

A trust once given

Shattered by deceit

Your words were sweet

But actions incomplete

The Silent Knife

The silent knife that cut so deep

Left the scars within the heart to keep

Timeless

Years may pass and miles may part,

But you, my friend, stay close at heart.

Time won’t fade what we’ve made true,

A bond that lasts, me and you.

Love's Pause

Your heart's been bruised

Your souls needs time

With patience and grace

Waiting for the day you'll find your place

Time

Time flies so fast

Age aging year by year

Here I am praying and wishing that

All of us can have another beautiful life

Roots of Love

Love grows deep where family stands

Through laughter, tears, and holding hands

Cheers

I walk my path, with heart and grace

A journey built, in my own space

Life


"Enjoy the little things in life, for one day you may look back and realize they were the big things." – Robert Brault

Appreciate every moments that's happen in our ife, as they often hold the most meaning in the long run. The "little things" could be anything by sharing laughter with a loved one, enjoying a quiet cup of coffee or a beautiful sunset. These moments might seem insignificant at the time but they collectively shape our happiness and memories. The quote reminds me to be present and cherish these simple joys because looking back, we may realize they were the foundation of a fulfilling and meaningful life. It’s a gentle nudge to practice gratitude and find beauty in the ordinary.
